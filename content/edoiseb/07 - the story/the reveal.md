#### WHY KINDLE TELLS HIM

- she's been wanting to be caught for years. [[quell]] is the closest she's ever come to being seen. being seen is what she's wanted, she just doesn't know it yet
- when she tells him, it's not a gift, it's a confession. it might also be a weapon: telling him to drive him away, to confirm she deserves to be alone
- underneath: she thinks she's contaminating him. tells him partly because he should have known before he chose her
- she'd rather destroy his fantasy than let him keep loving someone she's not. that's the love underneath the confession
