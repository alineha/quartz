#### THE RETIREMENT

- bresque is at the end of her run. she has not announced a date. she has chosen the night and not told anyone. [[orvaccio]] suspects
- when it happens: her final show stages a gesture the troupe has not staged in thirty-five years. the lampkeeper handing off, on stage, in the ring. she will light a final lamp, take off her coat, hand both to the next clown, and leave the ring
- the troupe does not know who the next clown is, in-world, in-show, on the night. she may hand the coat to the only person there who could plausibly take it. that may be [[kindle]]. that may be [[quell]]. that may be both. that may go unfilled — she may leave the coat on the threshold and walk out, and the next show may start without a keeper. she has not decided
- the audience will not be told it is her last show. the [[cinzária]] audience may guess. the [[the periferia interna|periferia interna]] audience will read it as part of the fable. she will not return for the closer. the show will end without her
- the next morning the troupe will know
