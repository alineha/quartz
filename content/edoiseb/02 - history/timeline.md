date system: years are counted starting from the çintálhi itself. "PÇ" = pós-çintálhi

- **~0 PÇ** — the [[çintálhi]]. electricity dies. the scramble begins
- **~0–25 PÇ** — the scramble. civilizational collapse, mass death, the slow horrifying discovery that conscious bodies are the only power source. the first converted are accidents. science is useless
- **~25–60 PÇ** — the battery system starts to grow. contracts, the "choice," state facilities, the deviation catalogs, second-class structure. science crawls forward. [[palâscia|brihčiá]] exists, electricity-nostalgic, running on battery labor
- **47 PÇ** — [[vow]] born
- **49 PÇ** — [[aldvor]] born
- **~60–89 PÇ** — late battery era. mature, bureaucratic, cruel, normalized. çintalhenses begin the hidden organizing that becomes the revolution. the world [[vow]], [[aldvor]], and [[kindle]]'s family grew up in
- **70 PÇ** — [[vow]] is 23, [[silv]] is born. [[aldvor]] (21, poor, already converted via battery contract) is the converted parent; [[vow]] still unconverted. [[silv]] is a rare "born çintalhense." [[aldvor]], still sane, understands what he passed on — believes he ruined his son's chance at a normal life. the guilt never resolves; his mind goes before it can
- **73 PÇ** — [[kindle]] born
- **78 PÇ** — [[quell]] born. NOT [[çintalhenses|çintalhense]]. nobody can explain why [[silv]] and not [[quell]] — the born-[[çintalhenses|çintalhense]] effect is rare and not understood anyway
- **~78–89 PÇ** — chaos/brewing years. [[vow]] increasingly pulled into the movement. [[aldvor]]'s decline accelerates; functionally gone by the late part. [[silv]] (8 years older) becomes [[quell]]'s true parental figure and hero while [[vow]] is absent organizing
- **89 PÇ** — [[vow]] converts (42), one year before the revolution, to organize from within
- **90 PÇ** — the revolution. [[silv]] is 20, [[quell]] is 12, [[kindle]] is 17. [[silv]] fights against [[vow]]'s wishes, dies protecting [[aldvor]] and 12-year-old [[quell]]. the [[palâscia]] empire consolidates out of the aftermath
- **~90–100 PÇ** — post-revolution. empire annexes neighbors. the state mythologizes [[silv]] as silvarèn. [[vow]] rises to general. [[quell]] grows up aristocracy in the new order
- **94 PÇ** — [[quell]] converts at 16 ("[[vow]]'s birthday gift")
- **~97–98 PÇ** — [[quell]]'s break begins (~19–20). small rebellions, then the [[l'cirqur lhêvora|circus]]
- **100 PÇ** — story present. [[quell]] 22 (a couple years at the circus). [[kindle]] 27. [[vow]] 53. [[aldvor]] 51, mind long gone (absent ~15–20 years). [[silv]] dead 10 years; the state owns his memory
