#### RELIGION • THE THREE DEATH-THEOLOGIES (TRIANGULATED)

- vitravora: souls reborn across lifetimes until knowing reaches vitória. conversion = soul deletion. humanity's holiness is a collective gnostic project. learning your way up
- căntalume: one shot. soul builds a light-shape; ascension = the shape made flesh (so conversion = the soul's successful fruition while alive). failure = fading. singing your way up
- éclisara: no journey at all. the soul is carried by the living's remembering. conversion is no event. everything fades. we are all eclipses. holding each other in the meantime is the only practice
- the same fact, three readings: vitravora and căntalume both believe their model correctly describes çintalhenses — and reach opposite conclusions. doomed vs realized. by their own rules, both internally consistent. éclisara doesn't bother — to éclisara, the question of what çintalhenses are is itself a vitravoran / căntalumen question. eclipses don't ask. they just keep saying the names
