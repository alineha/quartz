- the show has a spine, a fable across the acts. orvaccio narrates it, cirque-style.
- he reads the room nightly. louder in cinzária. softer where agents sit. the narration is the resistance, modulated live
- loaded device: the told-story can mirror/counterpoint the real plot. think pathologic style.

### THE SHOW

- 7 acts, one fable. each act corresponds to a story beat.
- two readings: one politically charged, one softer. every night [[orvaccio]] has to gamble whether or not to tell the _real_ story and risk having the [[l'cirqur lhêvora|circus]] shut down
- the visual signature: many hanging lamps, amber-glassed (see the lampkeeper's signal [[laws|law]]). fairy lights threaded across the rigging, the seating, the tent-seams. lit by [[çintalhenses]] — the lamps glow because of their bodies. the audience sits inside light made of [[conversion mechanisms|conversion]]. the visual is the thesis before [[orvaccio]] says a word
- the band runs continuous under everything. [[mavrenne, yulic and branno|mavrenne]] leads. they read the room nightly. [[the periferia interna|periferia interna]] = sweeter. [[cinzária]] = closer to the throat
- [[kindle]] is the recurring jester. she interrupts. comments. sets things up between acts. the comic connective tissue. the audience does not yet know what she is
- [[quell]] and other [[çintalhenses]] light the show. [[quell]] is rarely in the ring proper — backstage, threading the lamps, modulating the flow. the lighting design is his act
- [[bresque]] is the main role-- the lampkeeper. she has played this role for 35 years.
- she is on her way out. retiring. soon. every show might be her last. the audience never knows
- who inherits is unsettled. [[kindle]] is one candidate... the role might teach her into the woman she has been pretending to be. [[quell]] is another... the role would let him be visible as a worker, not as [[silv|silvarèn]]'s brother, not as [[vow]]'s son. the question is loaded. maybe even both, at the end of the story
- besides [[bresque]], the other roles (the initial child + the other children) rotate according to the acts. the initial child is [[wren and isk]] on act 1, then [[vesnê]]'s menagerie on act 3, and so on.

#### THE OPENING

- [[bresque]] opens the show. she walks the ring in low light, one lamp lit
- she greets the audience in her own register — warm, comic, the only voice the audience trusts immediately
- she lights the first lamp herself. with her hands. the gesture is small and the audience does not yet know what it is
- she hands the show to [[orvaccio]]. a century of clowns and ringmasters have done this handoff. we are watching one of the last times she will do it
- then [[orvaccio]] takes the narration, and the fable begins

#### ACT 1 (dusk in the forest)

fable: a child wanders, lost, cold, the dark is coming.

- the child = [[wren and isk]]
- [[vesyl]] is the forest. their contortion becomes the canopy. bodies as branches — low, then high, then a thing that shouldn't be a tree at all. the strangeness of the world first
- [[wren and isk]] are the lost children. small, threading through [[vesyl]]'s branches, looking for something they can't name
- the band sparse. uncertain
- [[the periferia interna|periferia interna]]: _"in a forest, at dusk, a child found themselves alone. as children sometimes do."_
- [[cinzária]]: _"in a forest, at dusk, a child found themselves alone. as children, in this world, often do."_

#### ACT 2 (the lit window)

fable: the child sees one light, far off. walks towards it

- the child = [[vesyl]]?
- [[domari]] alone in the ring. card and coin and vanish. light tricks. small things vanishing and returning. a lamp appears in his hand that wasn't there before. he holds it up, very small, very far. the audience leans
- [[kindle]] interrupts. a quick jester bit. she tries to find the light, can't, gets the wrong angle, audience laughs. [[domari]] moves the light. she still can't see it. (the unconverted can't see [[lutrons|lutron]]-shimmer. most won't catch the reference. [[cinzária]] catches it every time)
- [[the periferia interna|periferia interna]]: _"they saw a light. far off, in the dark. one window, lit, where nothing else was lit. they walked toward it."_
- [[cinzária]]: _"they saw a light. far off, in the dark. the only one. one window lit, where nothing else was permitted to be."_

#### ACT 3 (the lampkeeper)

fable: an old lampkeeper. doesn't ask who the child is. doesn't ask why they're wandering alone. opens the door

- the child = [[vesnê]]'s menagerie
- [[bresque]]'s act. she is in the ring. the lampkeeper, embodied. sweeps the threshold, listens for a knock, opens the door to nothing the first time, then nothing again, then finally to [[vesnê]]'s menagerie
- [[vesnê]] + menagerie become the keeper's other children. the creatures who arrived first. [[vesnê]] works in the dim beside bresque, supporting — exactly her register
- an animal opens a small interior door ([[vesnê]]'s trained latch beat). totó toddles through and is loved for it. véu watches everything
- [[the periferia interna|periferia interna]]: _"the keeper opened the door. they did not ask who the child was. they did not ask why. they only said: you may come in."_
- [[cinzária]]: _"the keeper opened the door. they did not ask what the child was. they did not ask whether the door should be opened to this child. they only said: you may come in. the keeper has been opening that door for a hundred years."_

#### ACT 4 (the coat)

fable: the keeper takes off their own coat — their only one — and wraps the child in it

- the child = [[quell]]?
- [[bresque]] is the center of this act. she takes off her coat, slowly, on stage. wraps an arriving figure in it
- around her: the band + synchronized lutron-light wave. the [[çintalhenses]] give their light in coordinated brightening. [[fárlo]] conducts from the rigging. the unconverted give the coat. the converted give the light. [[bresque]], at the center, gives both. they are the same gift
- she shivers afterwards. visibly
- [[the periferia interna|periferia interna]]: _"the keeper took off their own coat — their only one — and wrapped the child in it. they would be cold for it. they did it anyway."_
- [[cinzária]]: _"the keeper took off their own coat. they would be cold for it. they were always going to be cold for it. they did it anyway. as keepers do."_

#### ACT 5 (the lighting of another lamp)

fable: the keeper goes to the window and lights one more lamp. for the next child. the lampkeeper's work was not the rescue. it was the signal

- the child = [[kindle]]
- the handoff is on stage. [[bresque]] comes to [[kindle]], who is in her jester mode, fumbling. bresque hands her a lamp. unlit. a small offering. the audience reads it as a comic bit
- [[kindle]] takes the lamp. the fumbling continues. she drops it. picks it up wrong. comic. the audience laughs
- and then the fumble resolves. the swordwork is real. the precision is real. the speed is impossible. she lights the lamp she was given. with the disciplined hands of a [[a vârsălhe|vârsalha]]. sets it down beside [[bresque]]'s existing ones
- the audience sees the mask come off
- the troupe sees something else: the keeper has given the lamp to the next keeper. the show has just enacted the fable's deepest gesture — the keeper makes more keepers — and the audience doesn't yet know that's what they watched
- only [[orvaccio]] and the [[the betrayal|betrayer]] know what [[kindle]] is. the act is the closest the show ever gets to telling the truth about her. she lit a lamp the keeper gave her. she didn't refuse it. she didn't drop it. the gesture is undeniable
- [[the periferia interna|periferia interna]]: _"the keeper went to the window and lit one more lamp. not for the child already in the room — for the next child. for whoever was still in the forest. the lampkeeper's work was not the rescue. it was the signal."_
- [[cinzária]]: _"the keeper went to the window and lit one more lamp. not for the child already saved — for the next. for whoever was still in the forest. and there are always more in the forest. the lampkeeper's work was not the rescue. it was the signal. the lampkeeper knows the forest will not empty. they light anyway."_

#### ACT 6 (the second child)

fable: in the morning another child has arrived. the first child gives them the borrowed coat. lights another lamp

- [[fárlo]]'s aerial solo. the slow, aching, scarred-wings version. wren and isk on the ground watching — the next children
- [[fárlo]] goes up. slowly. the lamps brighten as he climbs (the [[çintalhenses]] give him their light). he is alone in the air — visibly alone — and that is the act. the keeper who lost everything still lights
- [[bresque]] is at the edge of the ring now. still. watching. she has handed off. she is no longer the active figure. the keeper who taught the keepers, watching one of them continue
- the audience finds it beautiful. the troupe sees what's missing. [[soriel]]'s empty spot in the rig
- [[cinzária]]: [[fárlo]] flies toward the empty spot. a deliberate aerial gesture toward the absent catcher. the audience there knows. they don't applaud. they witness
- [[the periferia interna|periferia interna]]: he flies the new act. they read transcendence. they applaud. they don't know
- [[the periferia interna|periferia interna]]: _"by morning, another child had arrived. the first child took off the borrowed coat and wrapped the second child in it. and lit another lamp. and a third. and a fourth. and more children kept coming, and more lamps kept being lit, and the warmth kept moving."_
- [[cinzária]]: _"by morning, another child had arrived. as you knew there would be. the first child took off the borrowed coat. and lit another lamp. and a third. and a fourth. the warmth kept moving. it was always going to keep moving. because it was never going to be enough. and they were never going to stop."_

#### ACT 7 (the forest does not get smaller)

fable: more children come. more lamps lit. coats keep moving. the forest is still full of lost children. the lamps are still lit anyway

- full ensemble. everyone in the ring. all lamps at full glow. no center. the keeper is no longer the central figure — the gathering she made is. [[bresque]] is one of them, not above them. that is the resolution
- [[wren and isk]], [[fárlo]], [[vesyl]], [[domari]], the band loud now, [[the menagerie]] wandering through, [[kindle]] as both jester and disciplined, [[vesnê]] at the edge with the animals
- [[quell]] comes down from the rigging. lit and lighting. stands among them
- [[the periferia interna|periferia interna]] closing: _"the lamps were still lit. the children kept arriving. the warmth kept moving. and the keepers kept lighting."_
- [[cinzária]] closing: _"the lamps were still lit. the children kept arriving. the forest did not get smaller. the warmth kept moving. the cost kept being paid. and the keepers kept lighting. as keepers do. as we have always done. as we will continue to do until the forest finally takes us, or we finally take the forest."_
- then: the band runs to silence. one by one the lamps dim. the [[çintalhenses]] give back the light they were holding. the tent goes dark. one lamp is left lit. [[orvaccio]] is silhouetted beside it
- "goodnight."
- the lamp goes out. show ends
